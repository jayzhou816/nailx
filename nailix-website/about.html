Our Story

From Passion to Excellence

Nailix Nail & Spa was founded in 2015 with a simple yet powerful vision: to elevate the nail care experience by combining technical expertise with genuine care for our clients' wellbeing.

What began as a small salon with just three nail technicians has grown into a premier destination for nail care. Our growth has been driven by our commitment to excellence and the loyal support of our wonderful clients.

Throughout our journey, we've remained true to our founding principles: using premium products, employing skilled technicians, and creating a welcoming environment where clients can relax and feel pampered.

Our Mission & Values

At Nailix Nail & Spa, our mission is to provide exceptional nail care services that enhance both the appearance and health of our clients' nails, delivered in a clean, relaxing environment by skilled professionals who genuinely care.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in every service we provide, from the techniques we use to the products we select.

Care

We genuinely care about our clients' wellbeing and prioritize nail health alongside beauty.

Safety

We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and safety in all our procedures and salon environment.

Innovation

We continuously learn and adopt new techniques and products to offer the best to our clients.

Community

We value building relationships with our clients and being an active part of our local community.

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices and using eco-friendly products when possible.
